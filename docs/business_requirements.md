# Business Requirements Document

## Project Overview

This project aims to build an end-to-end Credit Risk Analytics Platform using Lending Club loan data to support portfolio monitoring, risk assessment, and executive decision-making.

The solution combines SQL-based ETL pipelines, dimensional modeling, and Power BI dashboards to transform raw financial data into actionable business intelligence.

---

## Business Objective

Financial institutions require visibility into portfolio performance and borrower risk behavior to minimize losses and improve lending decisions.

The objective of this project is to:

* Monitor overall portfolio health
* Identify high-risk borrower segments
* Measure loan performance
* Track bad loan exposure
* Support underwriting and risk management decisions

## Key Business Questions

### Portfolio Performance

1. How many loans are currently classified as Good Loans and Bad Loans?

2. What is the total funded amount across the portfolio?

3. What percentage of the portfolio is considered high risk?

---

### Credit Risk Analysis

4. Which credit grades generate the highest default rates?

5. Which borrower segments contribute most to bad loan exposure?

6. How does annual income influence loan repayment performance?

---

### Underwriting Insights

7. Does income verification reduce default risk?

8. Which home ownership groups demonstrate higher credit risk?

9. How does debt-to-income ratio affect repayment outcomes?

---

### Geographic Analysis

10. Which states contribute the highest bad loan exposure?

11. Are there geographic patterns associated with default behavior?

---

## Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful if it can:

* Process over 2 million loan records
* Create an analytics-ready data model
* Identify key drivers of bad loans
* Provide executive-level KPI reporting
* Deliver actionable business recommendations

---

## Expected Business Outcomes

* Improved credit risk visibility
* Better underwriting decision support
* Enhanced portfolio monitoring
* Identification of high-risk customer segments
* Reduction of future default exposure
